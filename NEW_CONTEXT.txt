Mental Clarity, Engineered
1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Product Name: Nafsi (نفسي)
Tagline: "Your mind, encrypted."
Target Market: MENA region (Primary: Tunisia, Secondary: GCC, Egypt, Levant, Diaspora)
User Persona: Young Arabs (16-35) who need mental health support but reject the "soft wellness" aesthetic of existing apps.
The Problem:
78% of young Arabs won't seek therapy due to stigma
Western apps feel infantilizing (pastel colors, cartoon mascots, "vibes")
No option exists for people who want serious mental health tools with serious design
The Solution:
A privacy-first, culturally-aware AI companion that looks and feels like a premium security tool or pro-grade software—not a diary with stickers.
2. DESIGN PHILOSOPHY: "CONTROLLED ENERGY"
Therapy requires stability. Modern users want vitality. The solution is deep, saturated bases with electric accents—not washed-out pastels.

The Rule: Darker than standard dark mode. Electric accents glow. No beige, no soft gradients, no "calming" pastels.
2.2 Typography

3.2 UI/UX Patterns
Onboarding:
No email required. Anonymous UUID.
Single screen: "What brings you here?" with sharp, geometric topic tiles
No soft illustrations. Abstract 3D shapes (glass spheres, iridescent planes).
Chat Interface:
RTL native (Arabic right-aligned)
User bubbles: Solid --pulse-cyan, black text, sharp 8px corners, glow shadow
AI bubbles: Glass effect (--void-elevated), --pulse-purple left border (3px), 16px corners
Typing indicator: Waveform visualization (cyan bars), not dots
Crisis mode: Full-screen --alert wash, white text, monospace typography for clinical precision
Mood Check-in:
Pre-chat: "How's your head?" (كيف راسك؟)
Input: Radial slider with haptic feedback, cyan arc filling
Visualization: 7/30/90-day line charts with --growth gradients
Desktop (Electrobun):
Frameless, 12px radius, drop shadow
Zen Mode: Full-screen, --void background, floating input
Global Shortcut: Ctrl+Shift+N instant chat
No green tint. Dark. Focused.
